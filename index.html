Cat Care

At Sassy Cat Club we care for our cats and provide them with the love and support they need. Come check us out!

Cat Center

Our center provides ample space for our cats to roam,play,sleep and eat comfortably. We warmly welcome visitors to stop by and meet our wonderful cats who are looking for their forever homes.

Cat Adoptions

At Sassy Cat Club we consistently have cats available for adoption and regularly host adoption events. We also invite visitors to our center to meet our wonderful cats in person.
